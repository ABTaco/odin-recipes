About the Artist

Ye commonly known by his birth name Kanye West, is an American rapper, record producer, and fashion designer.[ Born in Atlanta and raised in Chicago, he gained recognition as a producer for Roc-A-Fella Records in the early 2000s, producing singles for several artists and developing the "chipmunk soul" sampling style.
